Mastering Social Engineering Defense

Social engineering exploits human trust to steal sensitive data.

Key defenses against social engineering:

  • Verify Sender Identity
  • Inspect Links Carefully
  • Protect Personal Information
  • Question Urgent Requests
  • Use Complex Passwords
  • Enable 2FA
  • Update Systems Regularly
  • Stay Informed

Digital hygiene tips:

  • Tighten Privacy Settings
  • Limit Public Exposure
  • Monitor Accounts
  • Secure Connections
